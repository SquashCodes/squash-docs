## About Squash Codes
Squash Codes is more than just a company — it's the forefront of innovation on the Multi Theft Auto (MTA) platform. Born from a **passion to transcend standards**, we combine **unlimited creativity** with **technical precision** to deliver exceptional resources for servers. Our mission is clear: to constantly innovate and deliver an unmatched gaming experience, enriching every moment for players in the MTA universe. With Squash, **excellence is the standard**.
